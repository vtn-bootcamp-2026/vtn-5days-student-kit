## 1. Design philosophy

Viettel's visual identity reflects the spirit of "Technology with a soul":
1. **Pioneering**: Leading in technology and innovation.
2. **Reliable**: Building trust through quality and stability.
3. **Caring**: Putting people at the center of technology.
4. **Modern**: Clean, efficient, and globally competitive.

The design should feel **professional, energetic, and tech-forward**, avoiding cluttered layouts or outdated graphics.

## 2. Brand identity

- **Brand Color:** Viettel Red ({{ colors.accent-primary }}) - The color of passion, energy, and the national flag.
- **Visual Tone:** Clean, High-tech, Bold, Human-centric.

## 3. Color system & Typography

- **Background Primary:** `{{ colors.background-primary }}` (White - Clean and professional).
- **Accent Primary:** `{{ colors.accent-primary }}` (Viettel Red - Key highlights, titles, buttons).
- **Text Primary:** `{{ colors.text-primary-on-light }}` (Near Black - High readability).
- **Surface Muted:** `{{ colors.surface-muted }}` (Light Gray - Content blocks, dividers).
- **Contrast Rule:** On white backgrounds, prioritize true dark text and structural elements (`{{ colors.text-primary-on-light }}`, `{{ colors.surface-dark }}`) plus Viettel Red. Avoid pale gray text, low-opacity lines, or faint UI details that blend into white.
- **Breathing Room Rule:** Slides must feel spacious and decisive. Use generous margins, clear grouping, and fewer visual elements rather than dense panels or decorative noise.

- **Heading Font:** `{{ typography.font-heading }}` (primary brand display font for titles and major section labels).
- **Body Font:** `{{ typography.font-body }}` (Medium as the default slide body weight/size for distance readability; Regular only for secondary notes).
- **Small Text Rule:** Tiny typography is allowed only for sources, captions, metadata, or legal footnotes. Main body copy, bullets, labels, and chart callouts must stay medium-sized and readable from the back of the room.
- **Local font files:** `themes/viettel/04_Fonts/FS Magistral/` contains Light, Book, Medium, Bold, ExtraBold, and italic variants.
- **Font reference image:** `themes/viettel/04_Fonts/FS-Magistral-font-specimen.png` should be provided as a visual reference when using image generation.

## 4. Logo usage
- Use the modern Viettel logo (Red text on White background or White text on Red background).
- Ensure safe margins around the logo.
- Standard placement: Top Right or Bottom Right depending on layout.

## 5. Motifs & Visual Elements
1. **Connectivity Grids**: Thin lines representing 5G/6G and network infrastructure.
2. **Geometric Accents**: Red stripes and shapes to guide the eye.
3. **High-quality Imagery**: Authentic photos of Viettel employees, technology labs, and Vietnamese landscapes.
4. **Data Overlays**: Clean charts and numbers with red highlights.

## 6. Content writing rules for slides
- Default language: Vietnamese (English for international workshops).
- Voice: Strong, Confident, Collaborative, Practical.
- Clarity over complexity.

## 7. Final design principle
Viettel design is about connecting people and technology for a better future:

> Công nghệ từ trái tim - Cộng hưởng để vươn xa.
